InSite app

The InSite App turns your surveillance data into actionable information. A green map locator means “all is well”. Once your criteria of an alert is met, the InSite App emails, texts or calls you to give you notice of the alert. Once alerted, a quick click on the red map locator takes you directly to the problem and allows you to view the area’s video feed in real time.

There are a variety of analytics available to trigger your alerts, such as person on site, license plate recognition, gate open, gauge monitoring and more. In Video Surveillance there is no “One Size Fits All” solution, even though all systems use the same hardware.
